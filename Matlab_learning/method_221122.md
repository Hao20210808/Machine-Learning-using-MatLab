### sub2ind()

-------|--------|--------
(1, 1) | (1, 2) | (1, 3)
(2, 1) | (2, 2) | (2, 3)
(3, 1) | (3, 2) | (3, 3)
#
1st col | 2nd col | 3rd col
---|---|---
 1 | 4 | 7 
 2 | 5 | 8 
 3 | 6 | 9 
#
